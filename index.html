About Us

Luminous VI is a student research group from APEC Schools focusing on how TikTok can affect academic performance. Our mission is to raise awareness, help reduce the negative impact of digital distractions on students’ learning, and to guide our audience to brighter horizons to uncover their true spark. If you’d like to learn more about each member of our team, feel free to check out our members !
